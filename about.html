🔥 Our Mission

FIRE Planner exists because financial tools shouldn't cost money or require handing over your personal data. Every calculator on this site runs entirely in your browser—we never see, store, or transmit your financial information.

Whether you're just discovering the FIRE movement or years into your journey, our goal is the same: give you accurate, easy-to-use tools that help you make better financial decisions.

💡 What is FIRE?

Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE) is about gaining control of your time and building enough wealth to live on your own terms. It's not about deprivation—it's about being intentional with spending, maximizing savings, and investing wisely so every dollar buys you future freedom.

There are many paths—Lean FIRE, Fat FIRE, Barista FIRE, Coast FIRE—and our calculators are designed to help you plan whichever one fits your life. There's no single "right" way to reach financial independence.

## Why These Changes Fix AdSense Rejection

### The Problem: "Low Value Content"
Google rejected your site because it appeared to be just a collection of calculators without substantial original content. They want to see that your site provides real value to visitors beyond just tools.

### The Solution:
1. **Legal Pages** - Required by AdSense policies (Privacy Policy is mandatory)
2. **Substantial Educational Content** - The new FIRE guide and enhanced homepage show you're providing real value
3. **About Page** - Shows there's a real person/team behind the site with a mission
4. **Contact Page** - Demonstrates legitimacy and accountability
5. **More Content Depth** - Overall word count increased from ~5,000 to 20,000+ words

Key differences between Traditional and Roth IRAs

Feature Traditional IRA Roth IRA
Tax on contributions Pre-tax (deductible) After-tax (no deduction)
Tax on withdrawals Taxed as income Tax-free ✓
2025 contribution limit $7,000 / $8,000 (50+) $7,000 / $8,000 (50+)
Required Minimum Distributions Age 73 None ✓

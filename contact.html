Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide personalized financial advice?

No, we provide educational calculators and resources, but we are not financial advisors. Our tools are designed to help you plan and understand your finances, but we always recommend consulting with a qualified financial professional for personalized advice.

Can I request a new calculator?

Absolutely! We're always looking to add calculators that serve the FIRE community. Send us your ideas at the email above.

Do you collect my financial data?

No. All calculator inputs are processed entirely in your browser. We never see, collect, or store any of your financial information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How can I support FIRE Planner?

The best ways to support us are to share our tools with others who might benefit, follow us on social media, and provide feedback to help us improve. We're supported by advertising, so using our site with ads enabled also helps us keep everything free.

Can I use your calculators on my website?

Our calculators and content are copyrighted. If you'd like to integrate our tools into your website or use our content, please contact us to discuss licensing options.

Response Time

We strive to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). While we read every message, we may not be able to respond to every inquiry individually, especially during high-volume periods. Please be assured that all feedback is carefully considered and helps shape the future of FIRE Planner.
